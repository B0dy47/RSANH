What is the Course?

This Graphic Design course is designed to equip you with the fundamental skills in digital design. You will learn essential design principles, master key Adobe tools such as Photoshop and Illustrator, and gain practical experience through hands-on projects and exercises. Develop your creative vision, build a compelling portfolio, and prepare for a dynamic career in graphic design.

Course Objectives

  • Understand the fundamentals of graphic design and its various types
  • Learn to use essential tools in Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator
  • Develop an eye for color, typography, and layout
  • Create compelling visual content and branding materials
  • Build a professional portfolio through a final project

What Will You Learn?

  • Graphic design fundamentals and design principles
  • Mastering Photoshop tools and techniques
  • Technical tools for lighting, shadow, and color theories
  • File handling, print preparation, and photo editing
  • Developing visual identity and branding strategies
  • Working with Adobe Illustrator for vector graphics
  • Social media design and presentation techniques
  • Creating and presenting a final project portfolio

What Will the Course Prepare You For?

  • Launching a career in graphic design
  • Freelance or in-house design roles
  • Developing branding and visual identity for businesses
  • Building a compelling professional portfolio

Prerequisites

  • Basic computer skills and familiarity with digital tools
  • Interest in visual arts and design
  • Reliable internet connection for online classes
